Latest News

November 2025

Red vs Silver Match: Alumni Appreciation Event

The McLean Wrestling FAMILY continues to grow! Our annual event on Thanksgiving weekend was a big success. Always good to see familiar faces catch up!

Fall 2025

Familiar Faces, New Titles, Same McLean Wrestling Family

Previous roles as assistant coach, and one heck of a "team mom" - Coach Mike Herlands will now be captaining the ship at McLean as the Head Coach. Coach Chris Hunter overseeing the Girls program, but we are excited to keep the McLean Wrestling Family together.

Spring 2025

First Class of Varsity Letters for Girls Wrestling

McLean Wrestling is proud to have awarded the first-ever varsity letters for girls wrestling at McLean High School.

February 2025

2025 Season Highlights

McLean goes 3-0 in final quad, finishes 6-1 in District Duals. All-State Jacob Daly joins the Century Club with his 100th win. Will Lewey also reaches the Century Club milestone. Three JV District Champions: Matthew Ghorbanian (215), Rawlin Freeman (113), and Peter Volkov (285).

February 2025

Our First Official Girls Wrestling Team at Liberty District

The Trailblazers with Coach Hunter and Coach P. Sophomore Lily Kilgore wins her 2nd Liberty District Championship at 114lbs after competing against teammate (Junior) Kaelyn Mackay in the finals.

December 2024

The Marines Battle @ Battlefield

The team finished 3rd at The Marines Battle over the weekend at Battlefield HS. Champions: Jacob Daly (126), Phillip Davis (138), and Will Lewey (215). Finishing in 3rd: Evan Herlands (175), Rakan Sweidan (144), Emil Ragimov (132), and Charlie Garno (113) also brought home hardware!

December 2024

NOVA Classic 2024

126lbs Champion - Jacob Daly, 8th Place - Billy Arslan. Senior Captain Will Lewey joins the Century Club with his 100th win!

November 2024

Annual Alumni Appreciation Event: Red & Silver Match 2024

Another successful Red & Silver Match! The McLean Wrestling Family continues to grow and our alumni becoming more involved. We had 50+ alumni come back and a packed gym for wrestle-offs, captains announcement and showcase matches.

2024 Captains with Coach Jackson: Will Lewey, Jacob Daly, Philip Davis, Tavian Melvin

February 2024

2024 Liberty District Champions!

McLean takes the Liberty District Team Championship! Liberty District Head Coach of the Year: Ken Jackson. Individual Champions: Will Lewey (190lbs), Will Olson (175lbs), Max Kusano (138lbs), Jacob Daly (126lbs).

January 2024

McLean Takes 3rd at Battle at the Bridge!

50+ teams from Maryland, Virginia, Pennsylvania, DC, New York competed at Woodbridge Senior High's Battle at the Bridge. McLean had 14 wrestlers compete, 8 in day 2, 5 medals, 2 in semi-finals and the 126lbs Champion, Jacob Daly! John Drury, Will Lewey took 3rd place, Phillip Davis finished 6th, and Tavian Melvin finishing 8th.

December 2023

NOVA Classic 2023

8 McLean wrestlers make it to day 2, bringing home 6 medals. HWT-John "JD" Drury and 215-Will Lewey taking 3rd, 126-Jacob Daly taking 5th, 165-Easton Johnson and 190-Will Olson finishing 6th, and Ryan Curry finishing 7th at 165lbs.
