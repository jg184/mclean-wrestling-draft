Mythbusters

"Wrestling is Too Hard"

FALSE. Wrestling is demanding, but incredibly rewarding. Your athlete will be part of an elite group that earns everything they achieve.

"Wrestlers Starve Themselves"

FALSE. Wrestlers compete near their natural weight. There are strict rules in place to prevent unhealthy weight cutting. In fact, most wrestlers are among the most informed athletes when it comes to nutrition.

"Doing a Sport Will Hurt My Grades"

FALSE. Teammates help each other stay accountable and coaches check grades. As Forbes put it: Wrestlers Make the Best Employees.

"Wrestlers Wear Tights"

FALSE. It's called a singlet, and it's basically like Under Armour for your entire body.

"I Need to Lift for Football"

FALSE. Wrestling makes you stronger AND teaches you how to use that strength. NFL coaches actively scout wrestlers — there's a reason so many top football players wrestled.
