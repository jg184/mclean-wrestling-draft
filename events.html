Red & Silver Alumni Match

Every Thanksgiving weekend, McLean Wrestling welcomes back alumni for our annual Red & Silver Match. This tradition celebrates the McLean Wrestling Family with alumni appreciation, wrestle-offs, captains announcements, and showcase matches.

The event continues to grow each year, with 50+ alumni returning to a packed gym.
