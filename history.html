McLean Highlanders Wrestling Legacy

Century Club — 100 Wins

Wrestlers that have accumulated at least 100 career Varsity wins during their career at McLean HS. It's a difficult feat to wrestle a full season, even moreso to do it for four years, and much more difficult to do it with early and sustained health/success. We recognize these wrestlers with a photo/name on the new boards hanging in the wrestling room forever.
